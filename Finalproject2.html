Naser Alqseer's Assignment 7: Final Project

For my final project, I made a 3D printed planter. For my design, I landed on using the moonphases pictured below found here I used photoshop and illustrator to convert the jpg file into a dxf file. Then, extruded it in rhino and used flow through surface to create the pattern on the base (reservoir)

Final design 3dm

After printing the top and base, I tried to use OOMOO 30 to create a one part mold for the base so I would be able to cast it using plaster. I quickly realised that I did not have enough OOMOO and it would eventually break but I decided to go with it and still order more OOMOO. Unfortunantly, I did not get it in time and thankfully I had my contenginecy plan of using a 3D printed base as a back up.

I used the 3D printed based I used in the mold for my final assembly. I passed the cotton rope through the hole at the bottom, and repotted the plant in the top piece. the pictures below show the final assembly and the insides of assembly.
